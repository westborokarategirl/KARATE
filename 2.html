Tentative Schedule

My karate classes will be held in the meeting room of the Westborough Public Library on Saturday mornings. The class for kids 11 and under will take place from 11:00-12:00, and the class for teens under 17 will be from 12:00-1:00. Parents will be allowed to stay and watch, or participate along with their children. Also, due to allergies, we will not provide snacks, but I strongly suggest that each participant bring their own snack and water bottle.
